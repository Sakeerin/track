# Product Overview

## Parcel Tracking System

A comprehensive parcel tracking system similar to Thailand Post and Flash Express, providing real-time shipment visibility for public users and operational management tools for staff.

### Core Features

- **Public Tracking**: Self-service tracking for 1-20 parcels with real-time status updates, event timelines, ETA predictions, and map visualization
- **Multi-channel Notifications**: Email, SMS, LINE, and webhook notifications with subscription management
- **Administrative Console**: Operations staff tools for shipment investigation, manual event management, and exception handling
- **Event Ingestion**: Multi-source data ingestion from handheld scanners, partner APIs, and batch files
- **Internationalization**: Full Thai/English support with proper localization

### Target Users

- **Public Users**: Shippers and consignees tracking their shipments
- **Contact Center Staff**: Customer service representatives assisting with inquiries
- **Operations Staff**: Hub and facility staff investigating exceptions and managing shipments
- **System Administrators**: IT staff managing configuration, users, and system health

### Key Business Requirements

- 99.9% monthly availability SLO for public tracking endpoints
- Support for high-volume event ingestion with real-time processing
- WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance
- GDPR-compliant data protection with PII encryption
- Multi-tenant architecture supporting various logistics partners
